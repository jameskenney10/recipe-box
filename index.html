🔖 Recipe Grabber Bookmarklet

Drag the button below to your browser's bookmarks bar. Then on any recipe page — NYT Cooking, Bon Appétit, Serious Eats, anywhere — click it to grab the recipe and open it here pre-filled.

Step 1 — Show your bookmarks bar
Chrome / Edge: ⌘+Shift+B (Mac) or Ctrl+Shift+B (Windows)
Safari: View → Show Favorites Bar
Step 2 — Drag this to your bookmarks bar
🍳 Save to Recipe Box ← drag to bookmarks bar
On iPhone (NYT Cooking app): Share → Copy Link in the app, then open Recipe Box in Safari, tap + Add Recipe, paste the URL, and tap Fetch.
