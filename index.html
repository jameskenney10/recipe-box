🔖 Recipe Grabber Bookmarklet

Drag the button below to your browser's bookmarks bar. Then, whenever you're on a recipe page — NYT Cooking, Bon Appétit, Serious Eats, Food52, anywhere — just click it. It'll extract the recipe and open it here pre-filled, ready to save.

Step 1 — Show your bookmarks bar
Chrome / Edge: Ctrl+Shift+B (Windows) or ⌘+Shift+B (Mac)
Safari: View → Show Favorites Bar
Firefox: View → Toolbars → Bookmarks Toolbar
Step 2 — Drag this to your bookmarks bar
🍳 Save to Recipe Box ← drag this to your bookmarks bar
Works great on: NYT Cooking, Bon Appétit, Serious Eats, Food52, Epicurious, AllRecipes, Smitten Kitchen, and most recipe sites.

Paywalled sites like NYT Cooking: Make sure you're already logged in before clicking — the bookmarklet reads what's on your screen, so as long as you can see the recipe, it can grab it.
